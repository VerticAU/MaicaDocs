# Discharge Services

{% hint style="danger" %}
Please note, as of November 1st 2025 Home Care Packages are being replaced with Support at Home funding. \
\
In Maica, to discharge a Service Agreement beyond this will simply be done via a checkbox attribute on the Service Agreement which updates the Status.
{% endhint %}

## What does the `Discharge Service Agreement` Quick Action do?

The `Discharge Service Agreement` button in **Maica** is a Quick Action with a customised User Interface for concluding Service Agreement. `Discharge Service Agreement` allows you to conclude an Agreement, and triggers **Maica** to generate a Discharge Statement and updated the `Status` to `Discharged`.&#x20;

## Where do I find the `Discharge Service Agreement` button?

The Discharge Service Agreement function is visible at the top right hand corner of your interface on the Service Agreement record, as shown below.&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/discharge sa.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

## How do I Discharge an Agreement?&#x20;

In order to Discharge a Service Agreement, simply click the `Discharge Service Agreement` button to display the pop-up, as shown below. &#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 9.57.57 am.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Simply click the Next button, and Maica's automation will be complete the Discharge process of the Agreement, and automatically generate a Discharge Statement and update the Status.&#x20;

Once completed, Maica will display the following pop-up:&#x20;

<figure><img src="../../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2024-08-26 at 9.59.19 am.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
