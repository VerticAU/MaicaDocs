# Additional Details

## What does the Additional Details stage include?

The Additional Details tab captures any extra details of the Shift, as described in the below table:

<table><thead><tr><th width="222">Captured Information	</th><th>Description</th></tr></thead><tbody><tr><td><a href="../../getting-started/maica-key-concepts/checklist.md">Checklist(s)</a></td><td>This allows for the selection of relevant organisational <a href="../../getting-started/maica-key-concepts/checklist.md">Checklist(s)</a> based on the tasks to be performed during the Shift. <strong>Maica</strong> will automatically assign <a href="../../getting-started/maica-key-concepts/checklist.md">Checklist(s)</a> to your Shift that are associated with the selected Shift Service(s). </td></tr><tr><td>Shift Status</td><td>The Shift Status is a pick list allowing for the selection of the appropriate status of Shift being created. This enables the selection of the following values: <code>Scheduled</code>, <code>Planned Completed.</code></td></tr><tr><td>Shift Type </td><td>The Shift Type is a pick list allowing for the slection of Shift Type for the Shift being created: This enables the selection of the following values: <code>Booking</code>, <code>Group Booking</code>, <code>On-Site</code>, <code>Event</code>, <code>Consultation</code>. <br><br>Once selected, the Shift will display the colour selected in the <a href="https://app.gitbook.com/s/9selzjEx6KX7RYEawAVr/settings/planner-management#appearance-settings">Appearance Settings</a> for the associated Shift Type. </td></tr></tbody></table>
