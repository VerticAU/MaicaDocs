# Installing the Mobile App

## Mobile App Overview&#x20;

The **Maica Mobile Worker App** is delivered and lives on the official **Salesforce mobile app**, available for both iOS and Android. This allows you to securely access your schedule, manage appointments, and record your availability directly from your phone. In order to access the Maica App, please follow the steps below:&#x20;

## Installation Steps

### Step 1. Download the Salesforce Mobile App

* **iOS (Apple):** Download from the App Store
* **Android:** Download from Google Play

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (15).png" alt="" width="375"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Step 2. Log In to Maica

1. Open the Salesforce mobile app.
2. Enter the login details provided by your organisation.
3. Once logged in, you will be directed to the **Maica Mobile Care Worker App** interface.

### Step 3. Accessing Maica

After login, you’ll see the Maica home screen within your Salesforce App. From here you can:

* View your schedule and Check In/Out of your Appointments or Shifts.
* Manage your Appointments and Shifts with a variety of Actions.
* Log your availability or unavailability.
* Contact your organisation.&#x20;

For more information and detail around the usage and actions within the Mobile App, please refer to the next articles.&#x20;

{% hint style="info" %}
As mentioned, the Maica app runs on the Salesforce mobile platform. If you experience login difficulties, password issues, or Salesforce-specific errors, please refer to the official Salesforce Help Documentation or contact your organisation’s system administrator.\
